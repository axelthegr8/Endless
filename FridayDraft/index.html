Carbonara




300 g spaghetti

150 g smoked pork

Swedish butter

1/2 dl whip cream

1/2 tsk salt

1 chopped garlic

3 dl grated cheese

2 krm freshly ground black pepper

4 egg yolks



What to do:

Boil the spaghetti.

Cut the pork in to small pieces.

Fry the pork in a pan, together with butter

Whip together the cream, salt, garlic and half of the cheese.

Stir down the pork and the cheesemix together with the boiled spaghetti.

Sprinkle the rest of the cheese and some pepper on top of the pasta.

Ready to serve!
